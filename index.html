About Me

Building Quality Into Every Release

I'm a QA Automation Engineer with ~5 years of experience delivering quality across SaaS, insurance, and telecom domains. I don't just test software — I architect robust, maintainable frameworks that give engineering teams the confidence to ship fast.

What sets me apart is my AI-augmented approach. I use Cursor AI, ChatGPT, and Gemini to generate and critically refine test cases, perform intelligent root cause analysis on complex failures, and build AI + human hybrid automation pipelines — combining AI speed with human precision and domain knowledge.

I bring a 'question everything with AI' mindset — using structured prompting and risk-based thinking to surface defects before they reach production, not after. Whether it's a legacy system migration or a cutting-edge SaaS platform, quality is my craft.
